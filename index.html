Hey, I'm Hannah!

I am a soon to be graduate of Northeastern University with a BA in media studies and minors in political communication and cinema studies. Raised just on the Virginia side of Washington D.C., I have been steeped in politics and activism from my earliest years.

My drive to create social change led me to an even greater passion, which quickly became the most important driver of my academic and professional life: media. From movies to magazines and everything in between, if is has a message (which basically everything does), I am most likely obsessed with it. My motivation to master media stems from my fascination with how it functions as education, entertainment, and even advocacy while also spreading and shaping cultural expectations and parameters.

My quest for effective and persuasive communication has led me to student journalism and podcasting, as well as internships at a government office, a nonprofit advocacy group, an owned media consulting firm, a student support office, and most recently, The Boston Globe.

I will never turn down a good debate, so please hit me up if any of my interests pique your interest!
